## State Management

### NUT States
- `normal`: Regular NUT
- `klesha`: Marked as corrupted
- `chained`: Part of a chain
- `root`: Root of a chain
- `completed`: Task completed

### Kama States  
- `trapped`: Inside klesha (not visible)
- `emerging`: During extraction
- `satisfied`: Just fed
- `hungry`: Needs feeding
- `bonded`: Totem formed

### Chain States
- `building`: Adding NUTs
- `complete`: Ready for identification
- `identified`: Kama type selected
- `active`: Task in progress
- `successful`: Task completed
